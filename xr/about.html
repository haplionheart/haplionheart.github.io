About HAPL

Hey, I go by the name, "Hap".

I started HAPL because I have a passion for interactive media, which I believe is a powerful vehicle for delivering information. It can let us tell a story while letting the user control the narrative. It can also better educate people by presenting information in a way that's easier to understand. eXtended Reality (XR) takes this a step further by providing more immersive ways for users to engage with interactive media.

Creating XR content is still fairly new territory. The available tools, technology and methods of delivery are constantly evolving. However, as with most interactive media, what is constant, is that creating XR content is usually a multidisciplinary process. One which often involves programming, graphic design, 3D (modelling, texturing & animation) and sound design, all of which are skills used to create the XR experiences made here at HAPL. On top of that, extra attention needs to be paid to UI/UX, especially in a medium where standards are still being defined and user expectations haven't been set yet. With that in mind, everything made at HAPL is intentionally designed with ease of use and clear communication in mind.

HAPL's primarily focus is currently on creating Augmented Reality (AR) experiences. This is because AR is currently the most accessible subset of XR, with many of today's smartphones being capable of using AR.

However, I think that XR as a whole, has a lot of potential.
